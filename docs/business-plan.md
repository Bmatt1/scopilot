### "What if I don't get any leads?"

Two things can cause that:

**1. Your geographic area isn't generating demand.** Scopilot works best in markets with active home improvement. Owensboro, Evansville, and Western Kentucky have consistent demand for concrete and excavation work. If you're in a market with no projects, no tool will help.

**2. Your scoping link isn't getting in front of homeowners.** We handle the homeowner-facing page. You're responsible for getting the link in front of the right people — job postings, word of mouth, local referrals. If you're not doing any outreach, you won't have leads.

What Scopilot does: turns every lead you get into a *qualified* lead. You spend your time on jobs worth quoting, not chasing dead ends.
